Our Story

From village farms in Tamil Nadu to your kitchen — no middlemen, no chemicals.

100% Farmer Direct

EcoWar360 started in Velachery, Chennai with a simple idea: connect village farmers directly to city households, cutting out layers of middlemen so farmers earn fairly and customers get produce that's genuinely fresh and unadulterated.

Every pack of dry fruits, cane jaggery and raw honey we sell is sourced in small batches from farming communities across Tamil Nadu, checked for quality, and packed by hand — no factory shortcuts.
